Air Alarm

Monitor and check the air quality around you, anytime, anywhere.

This is an app that is used to send alarms for bad air quality detected. It requires a bundled sensor device andconnects the device via Bluetooth. You can view the weighted overall score of the air and the real-time data and to access the threshold page for each environmental data. Also, you can view the real time data and view the data reference thresholds. Customize your the Alarm trigger conditions. The alarm can still be triggered in the background.
